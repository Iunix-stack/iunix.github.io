FAQ

A few things people usually ask.

How long will my website take?

Once we have your logo, photos and business information, a simple website can usually be ready within a few days. Bigger websites may take a little longer.

What if I do not have a domain yet?

That is completely fine. We can help you choose a domain and connect everything so your website works properly when it goes live.

Can I host my website with IUNIX?

Yes. We can handle the hosting for you from R175/month, depending on what your website needs.

Can I update the website later?

Yes. If you need to change prices, photos, wording, services or contact details after launch, we can help with that.

What do I need to send before we start?

Your logo if you have one, photos, business name, services, contact number, location and any websites you like the look of.

Will it look good on a phone?

Yes. The website is built to be mobile-friendly, so it looks clean and is easy to use on phones as well as computers.
